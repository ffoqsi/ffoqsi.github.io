Declaration on information obligation

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. Therefore we process your data exclusively on the basis of the legal regulations (DSGVO, TKG 2003). In this information about data protection we inform you about the most important aspects of the data processing in the context of our website.

Contact with us

If you contact us through the website's contact information, your specified data will be stored for six months to process the request and in case of follow-up questions. We will not share this information without your approval.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies.

Your rights

Basically, you have the right to information, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability, revocation and objection regarding your stored data. If you believe that the processing of your data violates against data protection law or your data protection claims have otherwise been violated in any way, you can complain to us via e-mail office@ffoqsi.at or to the data protection authority.
